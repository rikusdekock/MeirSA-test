                  Meir / Polished Chrome / Round Finish Wall Mixer

                  Round Finish Wall Mixer

                  Some items will be placed on backorder

                  SKU: MW03-FIN-C

                  R 690 inc. VAT

                  In-wall body not included. Please see /products/concealed-body-for-wall-mixers

                  Price excludes concealed part.

                   

                  A stylish method to control both temperature and water flow, the round chrome wall mixer is perfect for any area of your home, be it shower, kitchen, bathroom or laundry.

                  – Standard 1/2" BSP thread
                  – Handle 110mm
                  – Chrome Electroplated finish.
                  – 100% genuine European WaterMark Ceramic cartridge
                  – Washer-less technology

                  Please note, this product may have a visible Meir or Watermark logo.

                  - Lifetime cartridge, product, finish or parts replacement
                  - 12 months labour and ceramic disc spindles



                  Care instructions:


                  - Do not use any chemical-based or harsh cleaners. Avoid any rough surface materials such as scourers
                  - Only use a soft cloth with warm soapy water
                  - Filters and aerators must be regularly cleaned
                  - In hard-water usage areas, high-quality water softeners and filters must be used
